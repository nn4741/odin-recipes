Baked Chicken Wings

Description

These crispy boys are a quick and easy meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Chicken Wings
  • Butter
  • Kosher Salt and black pepper
  • Garlic powder and Paprika

Steps

  1. Toss the wings in butter and sprinkle them with your spices.
  2. Arrange them on a rimmed baking sheet with a wire rack.
  3. Bake them on 400 degrees for 40 minutes.
  4. For extra crispy, lower the temperature to 170 degrees for 30 more minutes.
